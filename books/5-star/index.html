My rating system

I follow a 6 star rating system.

  1. ★★★★★★ Had a huge impact on me, made a dent in my universe (6 books)
  2. ★★★★★ Learned plenty, maybe I will read it again (30 books)
  3. ★★★★ Got me interested enough to read it all the way through (35 books)
  4. ★★★ Neither good nor bad (15 books)
  5. ★★ Not so bad that it deserved 1 star but not good either (5 books)
  6. ★ Run Forest Run (6 books)

Unread books have no stars.
